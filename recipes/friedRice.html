Fried Rice

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made with layers of wide, flat pasta sheets. The dish is typically served with a tomato-based sauce, meat or vegetables, and cheese. Lasagna can be made with different types of pasta, including traditional pasta sheets, fresh egg noodles, penne, or ravioli.

The cooked pasta is assembled with the other ingredients and then baked in an oven. The baked pasta is cut into single-serving square or rectangular portions.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Sweet Onion
  • Minced Garlic
  • Cheese
  • Sauce
  • Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles
  2. Brown ground beef with onion and garlic
  3. Add marinara sauce, Italian seasoning, and fresh parsley to the pan, and simmer for 10-15 minutes
  4. Stir together ricotta cheese and an egg in a bowl
  5. Layer the lasagna
  6. Cover the baking dish with tin foil to trap moisture and prevent drying out
  7. Bake until hot and bubbly
